PDA'S OWN TEST SERIES

ABHYAAS

Practice. Analyze. Improve.

Regular assessment is the key to success in competitive examinations. Keeping this philosophy in mind, Pachim Darrang Academy introduced ABHYAAS, its own structured test series designed to help students evaluate their preparation, identify weaknesses, and improve performance continuously.

ABHYAAS includes chapter-wise tests, unit tests, full-length mock examinations, and OMR-based assessments that simulate actual entrance examination patterns. Through detailed performance analysis, ranking systems, and personalized feedback, students gain valuable insights into their strengths and areas requiring improvement.

Chapter-wise & Unit Tests

Targeted topic assessments after each chapter and unit completion.

Full-Length Mock Examinations

Simulate the actual exam experience with timed full papers.

OMR-Based Assessments

Real exam-pattern OMR sheets to build speed and accuracy.

Rank Analysis & Performance Tracking

Know your rank among peers and track progress over time.

Detailed Feedback & Improvement Strategies

Personalised guidance on weak areas and how to improve them.

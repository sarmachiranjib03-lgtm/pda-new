About Pachim Darrang Academy

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2010

Pachim Darrang Academy (PDA) is one of the leading educational institutions in Sipajhar, Assam, committed to providing quality education and shaping the future of young learners. Established in 2010, the academy has earned the trust of students, parents and the community through its dedication to academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development. Over the years, PDA has grown into a comprehensive educational institution offering classes from VI to XII, Higher Secondary education in both Science and Arts streams, and specialized integrated coaching programmes for competitive examinations such as NEET, JEE, CEE, CUET, NDA and other entrance examinations.

Our Vision

To empower students with knowledge, skills, values and confidence so that they can become responsible citizens and successful professionals capable of contributing positively to society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a learning environment that nurtures academic excellence, critical thinking, creativity, discipline and character. We strive to make quality education accessible to every student while preparing them for higher education and future career opportunities.

Our Philosophy

At Pachim Darrang Academy, we believe that true success is achieved through discipline, perseverance, hard work, and commitment to a greater purpose.

"ক্ষুদ্র স্বাৰ্থত্যাগৰ মাধ্যমেৰে বৃহৎ সফলতা লাভ"

This philosophy inspires our students to look beyond short-term gains and strive for meaningful achievements that contribute to their personal growth and the betterment of society.

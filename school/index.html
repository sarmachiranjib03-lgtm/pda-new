About Our School

Pachim Darrang Academy's School Section is dedicated to nurturing young minds during the most important years of their academic journey. Catering to students from Classes VI to X, the section focuses on building strong conceptual understanding, discipline, confidence, and lifelong learning habits.

With Assamese as the medium of instruction, we provide a supportive learning environment where students can understand concepts effectively while developing essential academic and life skills. Our objective is not only to help students achieve excellent results in board examinations but also to prepare them for future challenges in Higher Secondary education and beyond.

Our Goal

"To create confident, disciplined, and responsible learners who are prepared for academic success and lifelong growth."

Our Approach

At PDA, we believe that every student has unique abilities and potential. Through dedicated mentoring, regular assessments, and student-centered teaching practices, we help learners grow academically, socially, and morally. We focus on:

  • ✔ Strong Conceptual Learning
  • ✔ Regular Academic Monitoring
  • ✔ Character Building & Discipline
  • ✔ Leadership Development
  • ✔ Communication Skills
  • ✔ Co-curricular Participation
  • ✔ Individual Student Care

Why Choose Our School Section?

Experienced & Caring Teachers

Our faculty members provide personal attention and continuous guidance to ensure that every student progresses confidently.

Continuous Assessment

Regular class tests, unit tests, and academic evaluations help students track their progress and improve consistently.

Safe & Supportive Campus

A disciplined and CCTV-secured environment ensures students can learn and grow with confidence.

Digital Learning Environment

Technology-enabled classrooms help make learning more engaging and effective.

Preparation for Higher Secondary
